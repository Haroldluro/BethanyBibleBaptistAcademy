Requirements for Enrollment

  • Old Student
    • Report Card (Form 138 or SF 9)
    • Accomplished Registration Form
  • New Student & Transferee
    • Report Card (Form 138 or SF 9)
    • Permanent Record (Form 137 or SF 10)
    • PSA Birth Certificate
      • A clear photocopy of the Birth Certificate issued by the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA).
      • If the PSA copy is not legible, the applicant must submit, in addition to the clear photocopy of PSA issued Birth Certificate, a legible original copy of the Birth Certificate issued by the Local Civil Registrar (LCR).
    • Two (2) Recent I.D. Pictures (1x1)
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character
    • Interview with the Student Services Center
    • Entrance Examination
    • Entrance Exam Schedule
      • Monday – Friday (8am – 5pm)

Documents to Bring

(Enclosed in long brown envelope)

  • PSA Birth Certificate (Photocopy)
  • Two (2) Recent I.D. Pictures (2x2)
  • Report Card (Original)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
